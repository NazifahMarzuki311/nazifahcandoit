Family is My Happiness

Throughout all this semester, I feel very lucky because I was found with a classmate which is all kind, especially a friend of my group assignment. In doing group assignments, my friends helped me a lot and guided me as well as doing the assignments together. They also understood my situation while I was in a difficult situation and gave me words of encouragement throughout the semester. For me, they are not only my friends but we are like siblings who always help each other and can share anything. I hope that even after the completion of this diploma study, our relationship is still strong even though everyone will follow the path in their lives.
